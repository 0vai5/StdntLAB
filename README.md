## üéØ What STDNTLAB Does

STDNTLAB revolutionizes the way students study together by providing a comprehensive platform that:

1. **Connects Students**: Uses AI to match students with compatible study partners based on their academic preferences, schedules, and learning styles.

2. **Facilitates Collaboration**: Provides tools for organizing study sessions, sharing materials, creating quizzes, and managing group tasks.

3. **Tracks Progress**: Helps students monitor their study hours, task completion, quiz performance, and overall academic progress.

4. **Organizes Learning**: Centralizes all study-related activities in one place - from group management to session scheduling to material sharing.

5. **Enhances Productivity**: Streamlines the study process with task management, deadline tracking, and organized study sessions.

6. **Builds Community**: Creates a supportive learning environment where students can collaborate, share knowledge, and achieve their academic goals together.
